Welcome

Welcome to the Foy Lab! We are part of the Department of Laboratory Medicine & Pathology, at the University of Washington. Hospitals collect so much data through lab tests, and we want to use it better. We build computational tools to improve patient care, save clinicians time, and to learn physiology. Our approaches intersect math modelling and machine learning with large-scale, multi-modal clinical data. We work on problems across medicine, but are particularly focused on hematology, cardiology, and critical care.
