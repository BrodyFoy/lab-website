Brody H Foy, DPhil
Assistant Professor

I’m a junior faculty member in the Department of Laboratory Medicine & Pathology, at the University of Washington. My research is focused on how we can better use clinical lab testing to learn physiology, develop novel biomarkers, and improve patient care. We develop novel computational frameworks to handle multi-modal data, and develop implementable clinical tools. Read about my lab here!

Outside of research I have a wide array of interests. I used to run a non-profit AI consultancy. We worked with the NYC govt, Google Jigsaw, multi-national health insurers, and many more! I’ve also worked in suicide prevention, STEM outreach, and ran a policy think-tank. I love to play guitar, am obsessed with tennis, and adore TTRPGs.
